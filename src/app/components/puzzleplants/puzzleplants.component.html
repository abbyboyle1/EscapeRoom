Botanic Gardens

You emerge from the station to find yourself in a dense undergrowth of plants and fungi.

They appear to have escaped from the Botanic Gardens glass houses and are in the process of colonising what little remains of Byres Road.

The whole area is overgrown but at least the Plants are keeping the Zombies away. Before proceeding, you need to collect five edible plants from the following list:
