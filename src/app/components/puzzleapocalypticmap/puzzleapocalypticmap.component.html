You are in Glasgow Central - Very Low Level

You suddenly emerge through a hole in the wall onto a station platform.

You realise you are under Glasgow Central but now platform you have ever seen.


However, it looks like someone has left some kind of message - pinned at eye level where you can't miss it is a subway map .. a rather apocalyptic one.


As you study the map you wonder whether what looks like graffiti might actually contain a hidden message.

Examining the map, you notice that some letters have been added and some scored out to give:


  • St. Enoch
  • Bridge Street (changed to "Braaaiins Street")
  • West Street (changed to "Infest Street")
  • Shields Road
  • Kinning Park (changed to "Killing Park")
  • Cessnock (changed to "Cesshock")
  • Ibrox (changed to "Diebrox")
  • Govan (changed to "Gorevan")
  • Partick (changed to "Panick")
  • Kevinhall (changed to "Kevinhell")
  • Hillhead
  • Kelvinbridge
  • St. George's Cross
  • Cowcaddens (changed to "HowSadEnds")
  • Buchanan Street
  • Inner Circle (changed to "Dinner Circle")

Taking the 23 added letters, then removing 9 of the deleted letters, can you rearrange the 14 remaining letters to find the safe subway station?
