Tunnel

You follow the disused railway tunnel from the old Botanic Gardens Station to Kelvingrove Park.


From here, there appears to be two options:


  1. take the next tunnel south
  2. wait for a bus.

Unfortunately, the tunnel entrance is secured with a four digit code lock.

The prospect of taking the 666A bus fills you with dread but you still study the vandalised timetable while you consider your options.

You notice that the time of the next bus has been obscured.


  • 1601 Glasgow Necropolis
  • 1602 Trongate
  • 1604 Glasgow Green
  • 1607 Clyde Street, opp. Suspension Bridge
  • 1612 Oswald Street
  • 1620 Hope Street
  • 1633 Charing Cross
  • xxxx Eldon Street, opp. Park

There is something strange about these bus times.

Surely the four digit security code wouldn't be the time of the next bus?
