The sub-basement


The exit leads to some crumbling stairs. At the bottom is a long dark tunnel.

In the dim glow of the emergency lighting you notice a box of assorted items:

  • Post-it notes and a sharpie
  • a packet of salt and vinegar crisps
  • a caramel wafer
  • a half bottle of ginger
  • a lifesize cardboard cutout of Nicolas Cage
  • a hard hat with a head torch

You pick up the items which may come in handy.


Do you want to search the tunnel or are you too feart?
